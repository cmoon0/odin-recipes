Roasted Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 1kg tomatos
  • 3 small onions
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon tomato puree
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Quater all the tomatos and add to a rimmed baking tray. Drizzle with oil and make sure they are coated. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Roast in the Oven at 180°C for at least 20 minutes or until the skin peels.
  3. While the tomatos roast, slice all the onions and garlic and add to a pot on low heat. Drizzle in oil and begin to caramelise until the tomatos are done.
  4. Once the tomatoes are soft, pour into the pot with the onions, making sure to get all the juices from the baking tray.
  5. Stir together with the tomato puree and scrape up the bottom of the pot if any of the onions burnt on. If needed, add a few tablepoons of water
  6. Blitz with an immersion blender until smooth (to preference) and serve.
